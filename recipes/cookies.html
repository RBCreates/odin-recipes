Description:

The Levain Cookie is a mass of chocolate and cookie goodness. It's significantly thicker and chewier than a regular cookie and oozes chocolate with every bite.

Ingredients:

  • 113g unsalted butter
  • 113g brown sugar
  • 100g white sugar
  • 15g vanilla extract
  • 8g kosher salt
  • 7g baking powder
  • 4g baking soda
  • pinch of granulated nutmeg
  • 2 eggs (Straight from the fridge)
  • 280g All-Purpose flour
  • 425g chocolate chips
  • 240g raw walnut pieces

Instructions:

  1. Combine butter, light brown sugar, white sugar, vanilla extract, salt, baking powder, baking soda, and nutmeg in the bowl of a stand mixer fitted with a paddle attachment.
  2. Mix on low to moisten, then increase speed to medium and beat until soft, fluffy, and pale, about 8 minutes; halfway through, pause to scrape bowl and beater with a flexible spatula. With mixer running, add eggs one at a time, letting each incorporate fully before adding the next. Reduce speed to low, then add the flour all at once. When flour is incorporated, add chocolate chips and nuts and keep mixing until dough is homogeneous.
  3. Divide the dough into 180g circular portions, wrap with plastic and refrigerate for 12 hours minimum before baking.
  4. Set the oven rack into the middle position and preheat the oven to 350Â°F.
  5. Once the oven heats up, in a sheet pan, place parchment paper and 4 portions of cold cookie dough. Bake for 22 minutes or until lightly brown.
