Cheesecake Bites

Description:

Mini cheesecakes are almost too good to be true! When decorated they truly look dazzling on the table and theyâ€™re so delicious. Perfectly sweet and creamy cakes with a crunchy base filled with toasted pecans make for an amazing treat.

Ingredients:

Crust

  • 16 Graham Crackers
  • 35g toasted pecans
  • 56g unsalted butter (melted)
  • 24g sugar
  • 1g cinnamon

Cheesecake

  • 454g cream cheese (Room temperature)
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 200g sugar
  • 5mL vanilla extract
  • 2 eggs (Room temperature)
  • 2 egg yolks
  • 1/3 cup sour cream

Instructions:

Making the Crust:

  1. Preheat oven to 350F. Line cupcake tins with papers.
  2. In a food processor, mix graham crackers and pecans until broken into small pieces. Add cinnamon and sugar and pulse repeatedly until fully incorporated. Gradually pour the melted butter while pulsing the processor.
  3. Transfer a tablespoon of the mixture into each tin and pat down into a flat layer
  4. Bake for 8 minutes and set aside to cool.

Making the filling:

  1. In a stand mixer with the whisk attachment, add cream cheese and mix at medium speed to cream.
  2. Add sugar and salt to the mixer until completely homogenous.
  3. Add sour cream and vanilla extract, then add eggs and egg yolks one at a time ensuring that each egg is fully incorporated.
  4. Bake at 350F for 18-20 minutes. Turn oven off but leave door closed and allow cake to rest for an additional 20 minutes in the oven. Remove from oven and cool to room temperature.
  5. You can garnish the cake with dollops of whipped cream, berries, melted chocolate, pie filling, or enjoy plain.
